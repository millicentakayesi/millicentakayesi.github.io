Covid-19 Pandemic; Impacting my Community through Design

By Millicent Akayesi • 2 mins read
Scroll

TEAM MLVA. Finalists for The Markets-4-All Competition

Markets have been beneficial to everyone; the Consumers, Vendors and the Nation. Joining the Markets-4-All Competition together with my team member, has been a way to address the challenges faced in our Market Centres. Especially during this Covid-19 Pandemic, our markets require special attention. From safety measures, to social distancing, developing ideas to address these issues are important to the safety and well-being of everyone.

Through careful thought process, my team and I developed our proposal using five main focus areas;

  • Affordability
  • Sustainability
  • Inclusiveness
  • Response to Covid-19
  • Adaptability
